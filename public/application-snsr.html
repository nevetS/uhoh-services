Welcome to Stanford.

To gain access from this location, you must complete the Stanford University Network Registration process or use the wireless Guest Network.

Note: The registration forms will take just a few minutes to complete. Upon completion, Windows and Mac users will be asked to download and execute the Stanford Network Registration Tool. If your machine does not have the latest patches, it may take several hours to complete the patching process. Once complete though, it will take no more than 30 minutes to gain access to the Stanford Network.

By clicking the Continue button, you agree to abide by the policies governing computing at Stanford.
